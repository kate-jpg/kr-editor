ABOUT

I'm a freelance offline video editor born but not raised in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Since a young age, I've moved from place to place; Bangkok, Riyadh, Amsterdam, Guangzhou... I found my passion in film editing during my Bachelors in Film and TV Production in Dublin, Ireland where I also started working as an editor for local Irish companies clipping for catalogues and cutting commercials for social media.

After four years of study and work in Dublin-and a detour in Bern-I moved to the UK to propel my career into the film industry and pursue a MA in Editing at the National Film and Television School.
